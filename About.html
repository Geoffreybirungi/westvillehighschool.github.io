About Us

Our School's Background information

West Ville High School is a Mixed day School. It was established in 2016 . The school together has continued to expand the infrastructure of the school up to date; enabling the school to have facilities of three streams in Form one and two, two streams in form Three and four streams in form Four. The school has three Science laboratories, one Home science laboratory, one Computer laboratory, One Library.

Westville High School is currently a Sub County mixed day school. The school population has been growing steadily over the years having started as a single stream school to the current status with a student population of 1534 learners. In academic performance the school is one of the best public mixed day schools in Nakuru East Sub Country. In 2022 UNEB Exam, 10 of the candidates were admitted to the university. The school infrastructure has continued to improve.

West Ville High school

West Ville High school is a Mixed day and boarding School both 'A' and 'O' level.

The school was founded in 2016 offering both science and arts. Also famous for outstanding MDD, athletics and football. The school offers the traditional curriculum as per the Uganda national Examination Board. In addition to this, it has a unique program that provides practical skills and value based lessons that holistically prepare the learner for the changing world. Read more
