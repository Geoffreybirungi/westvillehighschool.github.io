The Director's Message

Welcome to Westville High School: A Partnership for Excellence

As the Director of Westville High School, I am thrilled to welcome you to our vibrant learning community. At Westville, we believe that education is a shared journey, one that reaches its fullest potential when schools and families work hand-in-hand.

Our mission is to not only educate but to inspire and empower every student to achieve their highest potential. We understand that the path to success in education requires more than just excellent teaching within the classroom; it necessitates a supportive and engaged partnership with parents and guardians

Thank you for entrusting us with your child's education. Here's to a productive, fulfilling, and successful partnership

West Ville High school

West Ville High school is a Mixed day and boarding School both 'A' and 'O' level.

The school was founded in 2016 offering both science and arts. Also famous for outstanding MDD, athletics and football. The school offers the traditional curriculum as per the Uganda national Examination Board. In addition to this, it has a unique program that provides practical skills and value based lessons that holistically prepare the learner for the changing world. Read more
