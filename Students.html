Our Student Leadership

Our School has a unique system of leadership characterized mainly by student leadership. From morning to evening , the running of the School outside the classrooms is mainly by the school prefects who have for ages proved to be very effective in their mode of leadership. In the Kitchen, all the proceedings are controlled by the prefects themselves. They serve one another in turns and incase one of them is not in for the meal his food is preserved. The cleanliness of the compounds and even of the boys and girls themselves is checked and corrected by the prefects. A challenge to them is that they must meet the conditions before they can check the fellow boys and girls. This is normally not a problem to them. The Westvilles force is hierarchical, from the Sub Prefect to the School Governor. In between them lie the House and departmental prefects, Senior prefects and Captains. All these are answerable to those in higher authority and should give them their due respect.

It's a humanitarian relief organisation.The society aims at mobilising the power of humanity in raising funds for emergency operations and disaster management programmes for Nakuru County and Beyond.The Society engages in the following activities:(a)KRCS Humanity power walk annually and (b)Visiting Children's Homes and retirement Homes

Computer club

Westville Computer Club is a club involved in training students about computers.In this Computer students are given tasks to complete and then they are shown on how to go about the task where they need help.Computer maintenance is also taught during the club meeting which really improves computer literacy leveles among students.

Patron is Mr. Birungi Geoffrey

West Ville High school

West Ville High school is a Mixed day and boarding School both 'A' and 'O' level.

The school was founded in 2016 offering both science and arts. Also famous for outstanding MDD, athletics and football. The school offers the traditional curriculum as per the Uganda national Examination Board. In addition to this, it has a unique program that provides practical skills and value based lessons that holistically prepare the learner for the changing world. Read more
